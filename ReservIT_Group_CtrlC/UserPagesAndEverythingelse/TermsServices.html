Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

To make a reservation, log in to your account, select the desired venue, choose an available time slot, and complete the booking form. You'll receive a confirmation email once your reservation is approved.

Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your reservation through your account dashboard, provided the changes are made within the allowed time frame (usually at least 24 hours before the scheduled time). Late cancellations may be subject to penalties.

What payment methods are accepted?

We currently accept cash payments, and GCash transactions for venue bookings. Payment instructions will be provided after your booking request is approved.

How long does approval take?

Approval for reservations typically takes 12 hours to 2 business days. For urgent requests, please contact our support team at support@reservit.com.

What should I do if I encounter technical issues?

If you experience any technical difficulties, please try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the issue persists, contact our technical support team with details of the problem you're experiencing.

How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion by emailing support@reservit.com with your account details. Please note this action is irreversible and will cancel any pending reservations.
